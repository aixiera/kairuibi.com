1. What GenPromptly does

GenPromptly helps users turn rough prompts into clearer, more structured prompts. To provide that service, I may send all or part of a submitted prompt, together with system instructions and relevant processing context, to third-party service providers, including the OpenAI API, to generate an output.

GenPromptly is an AI-assisted tool. It does not guarantee factual accuracy, completeness, or suitability for any particular purpose.
