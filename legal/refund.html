Refund / Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

This Refund / Cancellation Policy applies to subscriptions and paid services purchased for GenPromptly, a product created and operated by Kairui Bi.

1. General rule: no refunds

Except where required by applicable law, all purchases and subscription fees are final and non-refundable.

Because GenPromptly is an AI-assisted software service and access is made available immediately or on an ongoing basis after purchase:

  • refunds are not offered for change of mind;
  • refunds are not offered because a user no longer wants the service;
  • refunds are not offered because AI-generated outputs are variable, imperfect, incomplete, or not satisfactory to the user;
  • refunds are not offered for unused time, unused usage allowances, or partial billing periods;
  • prorated refunds are not offered after a billing period has started.

2. Limited exceptions

Billing issues may still be reviewed in limited cases, such as:

  • duplicate charges;
  • proven billing errors;
  • charges made after a properly submitted cancellation should have taken effect;
  • refunds required by applicable law.

Any goodwill refund or billing adjustment provided in one case does not create an obligation to provide one in any other case.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • the billing portal;
  • your account settings, if available; or
  • by contacting .

Unless applicable law requires otherwise:

  • cancellation stops future renewal charges;
  • cancellation does not reverse charges already paid;
  • cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period;
  • you may continue using the paid plan until the end of the current billing period.

4. Non-payment and failed charges

If a payment fails or the payment method cannot be charged, the following steps may be taken:

  • retry the charge;
  • suspend paid access;
  • downgrade the account;
  • cancel the subscription.

5. Account deletion

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active paid subscription unless the cancellation flow explicitly confirms cancellation.
