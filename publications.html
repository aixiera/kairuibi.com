Abstract

The paper compares classical CNNs with a transformer approach.

It compares established CNN baselines with a Swin Transformer for facial emotion recognition.

Summary Paper overview

Facial expression recognition matters in security, identification, and computer vision. This paper compares CNN local-feature extractors with a Swin Transformer's global-feature approach.

Using FER2013, pretrained models, augmentation, and multi-metric evaluation, the study focuses on practical tradeoffs.

Method Evaluation setup
  • Pretrained MobileNetV2, VGG-16, ResNet, and Swin Transformer models.
  • FER2013 benchmark with 48x48 images across 7 classes.
  • 20+ training epochs with augmentation and visual review.
  • Accuracy, F1, precision, recall, loss, and confusion matrices.
